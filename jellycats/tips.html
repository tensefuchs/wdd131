Care & Tips

Quick Care Rules

  • Spot clean first: mild soap + cool water + soft cloth.
  • Avoid heat: no tumble dryer or direct radiators (can warp filling).
  • Be gentle: dab, don’t scrub; blot to dry.
  • Comb the fluff: when dry, use a soft brush to restore plush.
  • Tag care: if the care label says “surface wash only”, don’t submerge.

Cleaning Methods

Surface Clean (most common)
  1. Mix a few drops of mild detergent with cool water.
  2. Dip a microfiber cloth; wring out so it’s damp, not wet.
  3. Gently dab stained areas; avoid soaking seams.
  4. Rinse cloth with clean water and dab again to remove soap.
  5. Blot with a dry towel; air-dry away from heat and sun.
Hand Wash (only if label allows)
  1. Fill basin with cool water + a small amount of gentle detergent.
  2. Submerge briefly; gently press (don’t twist) to circulate water.
  3. Rinse thoroughly in cool water.
  4. Press in a towel to remove excess water. Do not wring.
  5. Air-dry on a rack; rotate occasionally for even drying.
Drying & Fluffing
  • Pat into shape while damp; support limbs so stuffing stays even.
  • When fully dry, use a clean soft brush to lift nap.
  • For stubborn matting: a very light pass with a cool hairdryer while brushing.

Storage & Display

  • Away from sunlight: UV can fade colors.
  • Dust-safe: enclosed shelves or acrylic cases reduce cleaning.
  • Airflow: avoid damp areas to prevent odors and mildew.
  • Support shape: don’t stack heavy items on top.
  • Long-term: breathable bags (cotton), not sealed plastic.

FAQs

Can I machine wash?

Only if the care label permits. Place in a mesh bag, use a delicate cycle with cold water, and air-dry flat. Many Amuseables are surface wash only.

What if there’s a stubborn stain?

Try a tiny bit of diluted stain remover on an inconspicuous area first. Dab and rinse; repeat gently. Avoid bleach or solvents.

Loose threads or seams?

Don’t pull. Snip carefully and, if needed, reinforce from the inside with small hand stitches using matching thread.
