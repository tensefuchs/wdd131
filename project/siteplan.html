Jellycat Collector Project Site Plan

Site Name

Jellycat Collector — a personal site to showcase my Jellycat Amuseables collection, share care/display tips, and manage a wishlist.

Why this name? It’s memorable, clearly communicates the topic, and leaves room to grow beyond just a gallery.

Optional domain (not required): jellycat-collector.net (availability not checked)

Site Purpose

  • Present a responsive, accessible gallery of my Jellycats with filters (animal/size/theme).
  • Provide a wishlist page that stores items in localStorage.
  • Share short “Care & Tips” articles (cleaning, storage, display).
  • Offer references/attribution for images and resources.

Scenarios

  • “Where can I see all of your Amuseables and filter by size?”
  • “How do you clean and store Jellycats without damaging the fabric?”
  • “Can I save a wishlist of Jellycats I want to buy later?”
  • “What local shops (Düsseldorf) carry Jellycats?”

Color Schema

This plan uses the chosen colors below. Headings/headers use a dark blue gradient; body text is dark gray on a very light blue background for AA contrast.

  • Header gradient: Accent 1 → Accent 2 (white text)
  • Buttons/active: Accent 2 (white text)
  • Body background: Background
  • Body text: Ink
  • Links: Link (hover: darker blue)

Typography

  • Edu VIC WA NT Hand (Google) — headings & site title
  • Roboto — body text, navigation, UI
